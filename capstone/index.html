Wasatch Tax & Accounting Inc.

Welcome and thank you for visiting our Website. In addition to providing you with a profile of our firm and the services, this Website has been designed to become a helpful resource tool.

As you browse through our Website, you will see useful resources such as informative articles (in our Newsletter section) and interactive financial calculators (in our Financial Tools section). In addition, we have taken the time to gather many links to external Websites that we think would be of interest to our clients and visitors (in our Internet Links section).

In the tabs “Individual Tax Info for You”, and “Business Tax Info for You” you will find several PDF informational documents and worksheets. You can scroll down and find the various topics and clink the link. Feel free to print or save as needed. In the tab “Tax Organizers”, there are fillable (Google Chrome) or printable organizers for business entities and individuals.

To securely exchange confidential information and data, we use FILESHARE. Please scroll down to the FileShare link and upload your information to your client portal (as one large PDF rather than several single page PDFs). We will be notified of your upload and assign your file to our schedule within a couple of days of receiving your information. Once the return is completed you will receive a review copy to review and discuss as needed.

We accept all credit/debit cards, cash, non-temporary checks, as well as PayPal. Once you have received a final invoice, you can scroll down and link to PayPal to provide payment. The invoice # is your last name. Tax preparation must be paid for prior to efiling the tax return.

If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to call us (801)768-1500 - we’d love to hear from you. We pride ourselves on being proactive and responsive to our clients’ inquiries and suggestions.

PLEASE CALL THE OFFICE at 801-768-1500 TO SCHEDULE AN APPOINTMENT, OR IF YOU NEED HELP WITH UPLOADING YOUR TAX INFORMATION TO FILESHARE. (As much as we would like to see you - but in effort to keep everyone healthy - please do not visit us if you are ill or have symptoms of COVID. An office appointment is not required to get your tax return prepared).
